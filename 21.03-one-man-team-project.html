About

The curriculum of SoftUni is a combination of courses in programming and technology, entirely aimed at absorption of fundamental knowledge, developing logical and algorithmic thinking and acquisition practical skills with the latest software technologies. Forms of training SoftUni are present and online. The curriculum provides a choice of 4 jobs oriented around technology C #, Java, JavaScript and PHP, like any profession consists of modules duration of 4 months and each module combines 2 or 3 courses. The training courses are zanyatieya divided into lectures and exercises, each course ends with a practical examination.
